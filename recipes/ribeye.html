The Reverse Sear Method

The Reverse Sear Method is an innovative take on the ordinary sear method. Instead of searing the steak before finishing off the steak in the oven, the reverse sear method starts the ribeye off in the oven. After the desired internal temperature is reached (depending on done-ness), the steak is seared at a very high temperature on a piping how cast iron skillet.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak (~3 inches)
  • Unsalted butter
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

    • Optional

  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Garlic
  1. Coat the steak generously with salt and pepper
  2. Let steak rest in the fridge overnight
  3. After the steak has been rested, preheat oven to 250 degrees fahrenheit
  4. Place steak in the oven for ~35 minutes
  5. Monitor the internal temperatue of the steak and remove steak according to desired done-ness.
